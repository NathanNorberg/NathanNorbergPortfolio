About Me

My name is Nathan Norberg and I am a full-stack developer with extra love for the front end. I have always been fascinated with the ability to use technology to build new designs and create solutions to problems. This began at the young age of four with my discovery of Legos and has continued throughout my life. What drew me most to these blocks was how everything was built in steps and how each new section of the Lego depended upon the previous parts. This taught me the importance of following steps and directions carefully and thoroughly for the integrity of the project, big or small. Several times I needed to deconstruct sections because of a misplaced piece. Even though it felt frustrating to go back a few steps and remake the section, I felt immense satisfaction to have a finished end product that was made perfectly. As I grew older, my fascination with technology-enabled problem solving expanded to websites, video games, and other computer systems. In development, each line, element, call, or command needs to be exact so that the code can function properly and efficiently. When I code, the four-year-old in me comes out, and work becomes play. I love learning new skills so that I can become a more effiecent, valuable and well rounded developer...(learn more)
